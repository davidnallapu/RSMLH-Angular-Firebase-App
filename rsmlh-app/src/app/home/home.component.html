Welcome!

RSMLH is located in the city of Guntur in the state of Andhra Pradesh of India, this hospital was started by a husband and wife team of health professionals with considerable experience, to provide cost effective comprehensive health care with emphasis on Healing from Christ for the masses.

This hospital has been started in an old missionary bungalow situated in the Heyer Hall Compound of the Andhra Evangelical Lutheran Church. The Bungalow is about 100 years old and has been renovated. It was the place where the founding missionaries lived during their extensive work of building Churches, educational institutes and Hospitals. The last of these missionaries of the AELC was the late Ms. Ruth Sigmon. She served for 50 years among orphans and women in Guntur and the surrounding villages. The hospital has been named in her memory as she lived in this bungalow.

The hospital was established in 2010. Over the years in addition to the basic outpatient and inpatient medical facilities, we are now able to provide surgical, gynecological, medical and paediatric services. Patients have shown great interest in our unique method of physical, spiritual and emotional healing. We hope to expand our mission as we grow every day with the help of the Healing power of Jesus.
